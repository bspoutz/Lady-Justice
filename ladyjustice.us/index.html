Are there words more stirring, more fundamental to the American ideal? There is a reason schoolchildren learn and recite these words as they honor our flag.

We fought to secure and protect our LIBERTY as a new country. As citizens, we share in the benefits of that independence and self-determination. But JUSTICE has been a harder battle to win for all. The struggle goes on, from century to century, decade to decade, and sadly still today.

“Lady Liberty” has long stood in New York Harbor as a beacon of optimism and hope for all those who have sought liberty from oppression. It is time to honor the second part of the American promise.

Imagine “Lady Justice” standing on Alcatraz Island, welcoming travelers as they pass under the famed Golden Gate Bridge into San Francisco Bay. A shining beacon of optimism and hope for all those who have sought and continue to seek justice for all. Framing the country from sea to shining sea in the two beliefs we hold dear to our hearts. Lady Justice… Blind to the bigotry of race. Blind to the bigotry of creed. Blind to the bigotry of birth and social position.

My Fellow Americans,

I've had a dream for years of a new
monument erected in the San Francisco Bay
- that monument would be the
Statue of Lady Justice!

My name is Brian Spoutz, and I've lived in the Bay Area for nearly 40 years. I am proud of the innovative technology produced in Silicon Valley. But our country is still lacking - lacking Justice!

The 3 short videos presented on the home page, engineered by my friend Pascal Jardin, gives the 10,000 foot view of this provocative idea - that of erecting the
Statue of Lady Justice
that is as magnificent and glorious as her 'twin' sister, the
Statue of Liberty!


By placing this new monument in the SF Bay, on
Alcatraz Island
(what a juxtaposition!), will once again further the racial equality movements sweeping the United States. And the
Statue of Lady Justice
would signal to our far east 'friends' that our technology is NOT your technology! Countries will be put on notice that the US is your partner, not your innovator.

There are so many ideas that come to light when talking about building a new monument. How about having The Boring Company (hello Elon Musk!) provide high speed access via tunnels under the bay to the new monument. Or utilize the wealth of technology talent that exists in Silicon Valley! Engineers from Apple, Google, Facebook, HP, IBM, and MORE, could strategize & develop something marvelous.

I have many other ideas surrounding this project, but for now we need to get someone to take an interest in this project, and also provide funding! Frankly, I'd rather see the Statue of
Lady Justice
built instead of another single mile of southern border wall! Many Americans feel the same way.

We, as a country, already agree. Black Lives Matter! Sensible immigration policy matters! Racial equality matters! Making sure Justice is not pushed aside for sake of expediency. We no longer put up with racial equality.

Talk to your representative! Spread the word through leaders that will take action and build the
Statue of Lady Justice!
